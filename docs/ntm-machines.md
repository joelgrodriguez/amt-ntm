# New Tech Machinery (NTM) — Company Research Brief

## Company Overview

- **Founded:** 1991 by Larry Coben in Denver, Colorado
- **Headquarters:** Aurora, Colorado (16265 E 33rd Dr, Suite 40)
- **Second Facility:** Hermosillo, Mexico (opened 2004)
- **Acquired by:** Mazzella Companies (2015)
- **Employees:** ~87
- **Global Reach:** Machines sold in 40+ countries across all seven continents
- **Website:** [newtechmachinery.com](https://newtechmachinery.com)
- **Tagline:** "The World's Finest Portable Rollforming Equipment"

## What They Do

New Tech Machinery manufactures portable rollforming machines for the metal construction industry. Their machines allow contractors to fabricate metal panels on-site or in-plant, eliminating factory delays, transportation costs, and damage risks. Three main product categories: roof panel machines, wall panel machines, and seamless gutter machines.

NTM was the first portable rollforming company to commercially produce and market a polyurethane drive roller machine with separate forming rollers — now an industry standard adopted by most portable rollformers.

They introduced the SSP Roof Panel Machine in 1991 and the MACH II Seamless Gutter Machine in 1994.

## Product Line

### 1. SSQ3™ MultiPro Roof Panel Machine (NEWEST — Flagship)

The SSQ3 is NTM's latest and most advanced portable rollformer, building on the proven SSQ II platform.

- **Profiles:** Up to 16 panel profiles (standing seam, flush wall, board & batten siding — each sold separately)
- **Drive:** High-speed hydraulic drive for faster panel production
- **Controls:** Advanced touchscreen controller with job tracking and automation; optional upgrade to UNIQ® Automatic Control System
- **Use Case:** Commercial and residential roofing and wall siding — most versatile machine in the lineup
- **Premiered:** METALCON 2025 (Las Vegas, October 21–23, 2025)

**Key SSQ3 Improvements Over SSQ II:**

- Sealed drive gear covers with inspection windows for component protection against dust and debris
- RFID cover sensors that identify exactly which cover is open on the controller
- Shear warning strobe light for line-of-sight safety cue before and during shear cycles
- 8 strategically placed interior LEDs (work even when machine is off, LOTO-friendly)
- Improved bottom guarding limits hand access
- Slug funnel controls scrap for cleaner, safer floors
- Better visibility, faster adjustments, simpler maintenance overall

### 2. SSQ II™ MultiPro Roof Panel Machine (Predecessor to SSQ3)

NTM's proven workhorse and previously the most popular machine in the lineup.

- **Profiles:** Up to 16 profile options (standing seam roof + wall panels + board & batten)
- **Speed:** Up to ~75 feet per minute
- **Coil Width:** 15" to 30"
- **Max Coil Diameter:** 32" (overhead reel rack) or 45" (free-standing decoiler)
- **Controllers:** Standard PLC controller OR optional UNIQ® Automatic Control System
- **Power:** Quick-Change Power Pack (QCPP) — switch between gas and electric in the field
- **Optional Add-ons:** Notching and angle cutting functions
- **Certifications:** Can run UL-rated panels
- **Profile Changeover:** ~45 minutes
- **Best For:** Contractors doing both commercial and residential projects who need maximum versatility
- **Lead Time:** 20–22 weeks

---

### 3. SSH™ MultiPro Roof Panel Machine (Mid-Tier)

A strong mid-level option for residential and light commercial work.

- **Profiles:** 7 panel profiles available
- **Speed:** Up to ~60 feet per minute
- **Coil Width:** 14" to 24"
- **Max Coil Diameter:** 32" (overhead reel rack) or 45" (free-standing decoiler)
- **Shear:** Hydraulic drive & shear (automated, not manual)
- **Materials Supported:**
  - Painted steel / Galvalume / galvanized: 26-ga to 24-ga
  - Copper: 16 oz to 20 oz (3/4 hard)
  - Terne-coated stainless: 26-ga
- **Finished Panel Widths:** 10" to 20" depending on profile
- **Power:** Quick-Change Power Pack available
- **Best For:** Residential and light commercial roofing
- **Lead Time:** 20–22 weeks

---

### 4. SSR™ MultiPro Jr. Roof Panel Machine (Entry-Level)

The most affordable entry point into portable rollforming.

- **Speed:** Up to ~30 feet per minute
- **Shear:** Manual (more labor-intensive than SSH/SSQ)
- **Power:** Electric only (no gas/electric switching)
- **Key Features:**
  - Polyurethane drive rollers
  - Hardened stainless steel EZE CHANGE profile roller system with shear dies
  - Welded tubular steel frame
  - Length control limit switch for accuracy
- **Best For:** Contractors just getting started with portable rollforming, residential and light commercial
- **Lead Time:** 20–22 weeks

### 5. 5V Crimp Machine

NTM's exposed fastener roof panel solution.

- NTM's only exposed fastener roof panel machine
- Hydraulically driven polyurethane rollers with hydraulic shear
- Easy to transport — can go anywhere the job takes you
- Built for durability and efficiency

---

### 6. WAV™ Wall Panel Machine (Heavy Commercial/Industrial)

Purpose-built for wall panel production at scale.

- **Focus:** Heavy commercial and industrial wall panel work
- **Profiles:** 4 profiles on 25 polyurethane drive rollers
- **Controls:** UNIQ® Automatic Control System comes standard
- **Drive:** Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) for superior performance
- **Safety:** Cover sensors with LEDs
- **Distinction:** Currently the only WAV portable machine on the market

---

### 7. MACH II™ Seamless Gutter Machine

The industry standard for K-style gutter production, trusted for 30+ years.

- **Gutter Style:** K-style gutters from raw coil
- **Configurations:** 5", 6", or 5"/6" combo
- **Speed:** Up to 50 feet per minute
- **Max Coil Weight:** 1,000 lbs (reel), 400 lbs (cradle)
- **Key Features:**
  - Polyurethane drive rollers
  - Simple controls
  - Rugged steel frame
- **Lead Time:** 1–2 weeks

## Key Technology: UNIQ® Automatic Control System

NTM's most advanced programmable controller, designed to improve automation, safety, and user experience.

- Touchscreen interface with batch and length control
- Upload cut lists directly (integrated with AppliCad software)
- Built-in troubleshooting: error messages with help pages and videos
- CE-compliant safety features
- Controls start drive, notching, and shear functions
- Reduces downtime by enabling operators to diagnose and fix problems independently
- **Availability:** Standard on WAV; optional on SSQ II and SSQ3

## Key Differentiators & Selling Points

- **On-site fabrication:** Eliminates factory delays, transportation costs, and panel damage risks
- **Versatility:** Up to 16 profiles on flagship machines (standing seam, flush wall, board & batten)
- **Portability:** Machines are designed to go where the job is — jobsite or in-plant
- **Innovation legacy:** First polyurethane drive roller machine; continuous advancement since 1991
- **Customer support:** Direct access to people who work with the machines daily, no scripts or high-pressure sales
- **Retrofit-friendly:** New innovations can often be retrofitted to existing NTM equipment
- **Industry-best warranties** (referenced across their marketing)
- **Board & Batten profile:** Recently launched profile responding to market demand for farmhouse-style siding aesthetics
- **Global presence:** 40+ countries, all seven continents
