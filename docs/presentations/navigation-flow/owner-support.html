Action path · 04

Get owner support.

Existing owners are our most valuable visitors, and today the site mostly treats them like prospects. This path is one front door for everything they actually do: get help, run the machine, fix it, and occasionally buy another. “Get support now” is the CTA, not marketing copy.

Who’s here · what they’re doing

Asking for help

“Something is wrong. Get me to a human.”

Operating

“I need a manual, a part, or a training session.”

Maintaining

“Warranty, service, scheduled maintenance.”

Buying again

“Existing owner adding another machine.”

Decisions

Nav label

“Get owner support” replaces the split between Resources, Service & Support, and footer links. One label, one front door.

What we build

Owner support landing page, parts request path, repeat-buyer lane.

Risk

Without a real parts path, this label over-promises. Build parts or scope phase 1 to “service, training, manuals” and ship parts later.

Key takeaway

Owners deserve a dedicated front door. Service Hub anchors it, parts/manuals/training/warranty live here, and repeat purchase has its own lane back into buying.
