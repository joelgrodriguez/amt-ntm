Decisions

Nav label

“Get started” replaces “Learning Center” at the top level. Learning Center content shows up inside this path as action-named tracks.

What we build

Start Here landing, Roof vs Gutter explainer, business starter track, first-time buyer curated playlist.

Risk

If Learning Center stays as a floating bucket in nav, this path becomes a content dump again. The label and the sequencing must ship together.

Key takeaway

Get started is the only path where education comes first. Frame learning categories as actions, promote the readiness quiz, and always exit into Choose your machine.
