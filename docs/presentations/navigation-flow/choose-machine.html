Decisions

What stays

/machines/, /roof-wall-panel-machines/, /seamless-gutter-machines/, and every product page keep their URLs and SEO. They’re surfaced as the loud anchor in the flyout.

What we build

Machine chooser landing page (aggregates the quizzes) and a unified SSQ3 / SSQ II / SSR / SSH comparison table. Quizzes and product pages already exist.

Risk

If the flyout only shows the quizzes and hides /machines/, browsers will revolt. Browse lane has to be visually equal to the guided lane.

Key takeaway

Two equal lanes in one flyout: See all machines for browsers, Help me choose for the unsure. Browse stays loud — /machines/ and the category landings keep their place at the top.
