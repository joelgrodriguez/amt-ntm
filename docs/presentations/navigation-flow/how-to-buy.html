Decisions

Nav label

“How to buy” replaces a generic “Resources” bucket. It’s the dedicated home for price, financing, quote, sales contact, configurator.

Configurator

Stays in the IA. Moves from front-door to expert shortcut. Copy changes from “Find your machine” to “Already know what you need? Configure it.”

Risk

The homepage configurator router undermines this IA on the most-trafficked page. It must change alongside the new How to buy path.

Key takeaway

Sequence price → finance → quote → talk. Keep configurator as an expert shortcut at the end of the flow, not the start of every visit.
