# Phase 2 Brief — speak-to-each reference

Companion to `slides.html` slide 12 ("What's Next"). One short paragraph per item so you
can answer "where does this go from here?" without overpromising. Everything here is an
**idea / exploration** captured in the stakeholder feedback sessions — none are committed
deliverables with dates. Frame the launch as the foundation; these are the roadmap.

Sources: `docs/feedback/2026-06-03-navigation-search-review.md` (06-03),
`docs/feedback/2026-06-05-service-financing-review.md` (06-05).

---

## Sharpen the buying experience

**Compare tool** — An Apple-style "add to compare" that puts machines side by side with
their specs, so a buyer choosing between models decides with confidence instead of
flipping between pages. *Status: idea, tagged "phase 2." Source: 06-03 (Choose Your Machine).*

**Offer / promo banners** — The ability to promote something — "0% financing," a featured
machine, a seasonal offer — directly in the mega-menu columns and as per-machine banners on
the machines page (e.g. a "0% financing" banner on the Q3). Thad asked for this; it fills the
white space below the How-to-Buy links. *Status: idea, Joel to mock up. Source: 06-03 (cross-cutting).*

---

## Deepen ownership

**Parts e-commerce** — Let owners buy small replaceable parts (caster wheel, spring, handle,
bolt, screw) directly, without opening a service ticket. The payoff is two-sided: faster for
the owner, and it cuts the service team's ticket backlog. *Status: idea, long-term.
Source: 06-03 (Get Support).*

**Direct service requests (NetSuite)** — Today an owner fills a HubSpot form, the service team
gets an email, then manually creates a case in NetSuite. The goal is to embed the native
NetSuite form so a case is created straight from the site (and eventually the mobile app).
**Gated** on Thad's hard requirement: NetSuite must still track the *source* of the request —
if we lose source visibility, we don't ship it. Likely built via Seligo (NTM's integrator).
*Status: idea, under investigation, decision-gated. Source: 06-05 (Service request flow).*

---

## Future platform

**"Sally" support bot** — A customer-service assistant (given a human name/face) in the
lower-right, plus programmatic "talk to a specialist" CTAs inside educational articles, for
buyers who want to move faster than reading. *Status: idea / to build. Source: 06-03
(Get Started + cross-cutting).*

**Mobile app** — The longer arc. Referenced as the future driver behind direct case creation —
owners managing service and parts from their phone in the field. *Status: vision, no timeline.
Source: 06-05 (Service request flow, "and eventually from the mobile app").*

---

### How to present this

- Lead with: "The site we're launching is the foundation. Here's the roadmap behind it."
- These are **explorations, not commitments** — say that. Don't attach dates you can't keep.
- If a stakeholder gets excited about one, that's a good problem: "Great — that's on the
  list; let's prioritize it after launch."
